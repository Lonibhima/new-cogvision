About Us

Cogvision Pvt. Ltd., is a subsidiary of Cogxar research and development center for artificial intelligence which was founded in the year 2012.

Cogvision’s AI platform is focused on providing next-generation AI based video and vision analytics by optimizing and reducing the infrastructure needs (overall storage, compute and network bandwidth requirements) for our clients.

Cogvision’s focus areas would be to bring intelligence into Industrial Inspection using proprietary Mathematical and analytical models and providing Safety & Security solutions using advanced Facial Recognition AI models.

Headquartered in Bangalore, Cogvision has a strong techno leadership team with the combination of strong R&D in artificial intelligence and exposure to running large scale programs at Infosys, Accenture, Dell, Siemens, PwC, IBM, Thomson Reuters, Honeywell & Procter Gamble and building successful startups.
